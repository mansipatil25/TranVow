# Features Guide

## 🎤 Voice Mode
Press and hold the mic button, speak naturally, and release. Your speech is transcribed live using the Web Speech API, then translated instantly.

## ⌨️ Type Text Mode
For noisy environments or when you'd rather type, switch to "Type Text" mode. Type or paste your text (up to 500 characters) and hit Translate.

## 🌗 Dark / Light Theme
Click the sun/moon icon in the top-right corner to switch between dark and light themes. Your preference is saved automatically and remembered next time you visit.

## 🌍 20+ Languages
Supported: English, Spanish, French, Hindi, German, Japanese, Chinese, Arabic, Portuguese, Russian, Italian, Korean, Dutch, Turkish, Polish, Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, Bengali, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Gujarati, Urdu, Swedish, Greek.

## 📥 Download Translation
After translating, click "Download as .txt" to save the original + translated text as a plain text file, with timestamp included.

## 🔃 Swap Languages
Click the swap icon between the two language dropdowns to instantly flip source and target languages (and swap displayed results too, if available).

## 🕒 Translation History
Your last 20 translations are saved locally in your browser (localStorage) so you can revisit them even after refreshing the page.

## 🔊 Audio Playback
Every translation is automatically read aloud using your browser's built-in text-to-speech voices. Click "Play" to replay it anytime.
